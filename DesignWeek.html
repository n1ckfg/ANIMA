Connections

between material and virtual worlds

an exploration of media archeology

A series of startling discoveries in the vaulted walls of eccad's concourse gallery reveals unexpected connections between phaistos disc (c.2000 bc) and photo cd.

This exhibit explores historical highlights of the mediums and technologies we've created to communicate with one another - from the vernacular to the esoteric - and the innovative options for accessing that information.
