"What a beautiful hill!" They all exclaimed, enraptured.

" This hill, which at first shuts out the view, heightens expectations. If one were to see the whole park immediately upon entering, the effect would be monotonous, " remarked Chia Ching.

"Quite so!" the others agreed.

From "The Dream Of The Red Chamber" (One of the Classics of Chinese literature) By Ts'ao Hsueh-Ch'
