Vancouver Cyberspace Association

General Information

The Vancouver Cyberspace Association (VCA) is an association of arts organizations in Vancouver interested in promoting the visual, performing and literary arts on the Internet. Its goals are: to promote the activities of the arts through a variety of online programs, services and market strategies; to culturally educate the public online, and to increase public awareness of and availability to Internet for the arts.

The Vancouver Cyberspace Association is a member of the BC Cultural Information Consortium, and a major sponsor and active participant in ANIMA and ArtWorld.
